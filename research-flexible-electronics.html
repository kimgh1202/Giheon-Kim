Flexible electronics

Developing ultra-conformable and stretchable systems for next-generation bioelectronics.

Overview

My research in Flexible electronics focuses on creating seamless and biocompatible electronic devices. I explore advanced materials and fabrication techniques to design systems that can intimately interface with biological tissues, providing highly stable and longitudinal performance.

Key Explorations

  • Stretchable Substrates: Investigating novel elastomers and polymers for extreme conformability.
  • Biocompatible Packaging: Enhancing the chronic stability of chronically implanted devices.
  • High-Density Arrays: Scaling up microelectrodes on flexible platforms for high-resolution mapping.
