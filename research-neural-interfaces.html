Neural interfaces

Bridging the gap between the nervous system and intelligent computing platforms.

Overview

My work in Neural interfaces centers around translating neural activity into actionable data and seamlessly stimulating the nervous system. This bidirectional communication holds the key to treating neurological disorders and enhancing human cognition.

Key Focus Areas

  • Multimodal Recording: Enabling co-localized recording of electrical and chemical signals simultaneously.
  • Closed-Loop Systems: Developing intelligent decoding algorithms that drive targeted stimulation in real-time.
  • In Vivo Electrophysiology: Validating the long-term efficacy and safety of novel microelectrodes in animal models.
