Artificial Intelligence

Leveraging machine learning for adaptive user experiences.

Overview

My work in Artificial Intelligence centers around applying advanced machine learning algorithms, particularly Large Language Models (LLMs), to create intelligent systems that assist, learn from, and adapt to human users.

Key Focus Areas

  • Agentic AI: Developing autonomous agents that can plan and execute complex workflows on behalf of the user.
  • Trust & Transparency: Understanding how to build AI systems that users can trust and interpret easily.
  • Generative AI in Design: Exploring how generative models can assist designers and developers in their creative processes.
