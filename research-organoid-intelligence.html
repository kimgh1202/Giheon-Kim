Organoid intelligence

Harnessing the power of biological neural networks for advanced embodied AI.

Overview

Organoid intelligence aims to utilize 3D biological neural networks (brain organoids) as a novel computing paradigm. My research involves developing sophisticated microelectrode arrays and fluidic platforms to culture, monitor, and interact with these bio-computers effectively.

Current Interests

  • 3D Microfluidics: Designing bioprinted systems for continuous monitoring of glucose, lactate, and cellular health.
  • Bio-Computing Interfaces: Connecting organoids to artificial systems to evaluate learning and memory processes.
  • Long-Term Viability: Establishing reliable environments that sustain biological neural networks over months for prolonged studies.
